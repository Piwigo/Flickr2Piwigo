Duplicates

Photos that already exist in Piwigo will not be imported.

The comparison is done firstly on the filename (for a match to "flickr-<Flickr_ID>") and then on the checksum of the actual image file. Note that no metadata is updated for photos already imported.

Approximate dates

Flickr supports approximate dates for "date taken" attribute of photos.

These will be imported as exact dates, and an approximate date keyword added as well as keywords for the date (e.g. c. 1933, March 2008, or 1986) and granularity (one of: month, year, circa).

For example, a date taken on Flickr of "c. 2006" will be imported with a date of "2006-01-01 00:00:00" and keywords "approximate date", "circa", and "c. 2006".
